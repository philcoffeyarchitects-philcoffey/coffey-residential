Who are We? - High End Residential Architects London

For over two decades, Coffey Residential, High End Residential Architects London, has been a studio driven by curiosity, collaboration, and craft. Our team brings together creative minds who share a passion for design and the way people live. We work closely with clients to shape bespoke projects, offering a highly personalised and considered approach.

Our work is built on strong relationships, meticulous attention to detail, and a belief that great design should be both intelligent and intuitive.

What Do We Do?

Bespoke New Builds

Coffey Residential crafts homes across urban, suburban, and countryside settings. From compact infills to expansive coastal abodes, our collaborative design fuses aesthetics and emotion, reflecting your personality perfectly.

Renovation and Interiors

Coffey Residential revitalises London homes with elegant, light-filled interiors. Our experience in modernising structures preserves your home's original charm while ensuring longevity and aesthetic appeal.

Extensions

Coffey Residential boasts award-winning extensions across London—from rooftop and side additions to double-storey and basements. We ensure seamless integration, creating a harmonious home.

Personalised Design

Coffey Residential co-designs your dream home via an iterative process using advanced software, VR, models, and sketches to explore designs. Clear communication ensures outcomes that meet your needs and enhance everyday living.

Planning and Conservation

Coffey Residential brings extensive experience with planning departments across London and the UK, expertly managing Conservation Areas and Listed Buildings. Our sustainable designs respect context, ensuring smooth planning permissions.

Project Management

Coffey Residential nurtures strong ties with contractors and welcomes local expertise. From initial conception to final handover, we meticulously manage each project to adhere to your timeline and budget.
