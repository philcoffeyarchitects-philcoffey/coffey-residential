Light Reading - We Feature in National and Design Press

Coffey Residential has been featured in leading architecture and design publications, including Architects Journal and Wallpaper, as well as national press such as The Guardian and The Daily Telegraph. Recognised for our innovative approach to residential architecture, we create bespoke, light-filled homes that redefine contemporary luxury in London and beyond. Our work blends timeless elegance with modern craftsmanship, earning accolades and capturing the attention of the global press.

Explore how the world’s media has celebrated our award-winning designs and the ethos that drives our studio. From striking contemporary spaces to sensitive renovations, our projects showcase the seamless integration of light, form, and function. As one of London’s leading residential architects, we are proud to share the stories behind our transformative homes, offering a glimpse into the creative process and inspiration that shape each unique project.

Explore our selected press links below, showcasing how Coffey Residential and its parent practice, Coffey Architects, have been celebrated by leading architecture and design publications. As a specialised arm of Coffey Architects, Coffey Residential focuses exclusively on bespoke residential projects, benefitting from the expertise, innovation, and legacy of the wider practice.

This dedicated approach ensures the best possible results, with a team devoted to creating light-filled, luxurious homes. From insightful interviews to detailed project showcases, these features highlight our commitment to excellence and our reputation for redefining contemporary living.
