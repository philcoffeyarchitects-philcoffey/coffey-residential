Choosing the best architect for the job

Words · Coffey Residential · 1 min read

Make the right choice and count on Coffey Architects.

A Vital Role

An architect will come up with a concept, taking your ideas into account. They can then make this real with a design that takes factors that influence planning permission into consideration. This will make the process as smooth as possible so that building can commence. The skills and knowledge architects possess are invaluable. Each architect will need to work with the Architects Registration Board (ARB). In addition, many will also work with other bodies such as the Royal Institute of British Architecture (RIBA).

Talk To Them

You need to know that you can get on with your architect. In addition you need them to be able to make your dream a reality. Start by getting to know them with a discussion on what you want from the project. Part of this is creating a valuable space as well as offering suggestions on ways to make it as great as possible. Communication is a vital part of architecture. It provides value at every stage, from designing all the way to managing the project. We are always happy to keep you informed on developments and talk about what you want in detail. This includes answering any potential queries.

Previous Projects

A great way to know if the people you are working with are high quality is to look at their completed work. On our site, you can see this for yourself. We have won numerous awards from organisations like RIBA for our work over the years we have been operating. This is as well as being on the shortlist for many others.
