#### Making the most of the site

You might lose the chance to realise the full potential of a home if you make mistakes when selecting the site. What you need to do is consider the surroundings, including views, access, and nearby amenities. In addition, make sure you think ahead and imagine how the area could develop. Is it likely in the future that there will be major developments nearby that may cause issues like overlooking?

One of the most important things here is to do all you can to protect lovely views. They add a lot of value to homes, whether it is a view of a park, the sea, a marina, a skyline, or even a landmark. So make sure the views cannot be built out, such as choosing a site on an incline or a location where you can build a taller home.

#### The perfect basement

homes are often spacious and full of amenities like a gym, games room, pool, cinema, and spaces for any other hobbies or passions. Getting all the space for them can be tricky, especially with smaller sites and high land prices. A great solution is to invest in a basement. It can be costly, but the investment is well worth it in most cases.

When you are designing a basement, you need to think very carefully about what you want here. That will affect the design in terms of what you need to do about lighting and making it comfortable. If you want to maximise the potential of the space, design it with experts.

#### Natural materials

Nothing else says like natural materials. We're talking beautiful timber and stone. They provide a number of benefits, including providing texture and adding depth to spaces. More importantly, they age beautifully and don't release harmful VOCs.

The materials are really one area where you definitely can't skimp. If you try to, it can undermine the whole design. Even if you can replicate the look of natural materials with composites and veneers, something will be missing. Most crucially, you will lose out on the timelessness of the real thing.

#### Effective use of space

As we said above, space is really important in homes. Really, who wants a massive home that makes everyone feel isolated? What you want instead is fantastic designs for the spaces, ensuring they are comfortable, allow movement, and suit the needs of occupants. Crucially, make key areas places people want to spend time.
