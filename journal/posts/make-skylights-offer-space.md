This is inspiring to everybody who works here. Anyone who is planning a project can work with the amazing people at Coffey Architects. Having us on your team allows you to have peace of mind that the end result will be a spectacular one. We can work with you to develop your ideas and then suggest ways to make them as great as possible. A feature you might want to incorporate into your project is skylights. They can turn a fairly standard room into a bright, sunny, and gorgeous space.

#### Bathe In The Daylight

A skylight can let in up to two times as much light as you would get from vertical glazing. Getting more light in your home reduces the need for electrical lights. This makes sense as there is no need to be dependent on electricity when you can use what nature offers for free. You will also help the environment at the same time as lower energy consumption means you produce fewer greenhouse gases.

#### Promote Good Health

Countless studies have found that exposure to natural sunlight is beneficial for wellbeing. It can boost morale, lower fatigue, and improve productivity. In fact, various pieces of research show sunlight can stimulate faster recovery from illness.

#### A Space That Looks Great

Better light for your home will illuminate it, making colours appear more vibrant and enhancing interiors. The light in rooms will appear more balanced, glare will be reduced, and interiors will look more open. Whatever look you create in your home, a skylight can improve it.

#### Keeping Your Privacy

A lot of homes are built close together nowadays which makes opening windows difficult in terms of privacy. You won't have to worry about this though when you have skylights. You will get sunlight but they are on your roof rather than facing your neighbours. Working with us can spark ideas for your project in order to produce spaces that are perfect for the needs of the user.
