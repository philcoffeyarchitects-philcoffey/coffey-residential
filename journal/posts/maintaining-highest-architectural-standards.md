We are dedicated to improving the commercial, public and residential architecture of London and boosting the architectural profile of this fantastic city. We do all we can to provide our clients with high quality architecture and a service that simply cannot be replicated by anyone else. There is an ingrained culture of excellence that we carry throughout our practice, which has ensured our continuing outstanding reputation. Our culture of excellence drives both Phil Coffey and our team of architects towards completing work to a highly celebrated standard. Our talented, ambitious staff undertake any and all projects, and you can be assured that when you are working with us the work will be in the hands of the professionals. Our culture of excellence has driven us towards receiving many awards. We were a Double RIBA Award winner in 2012, received the Retrofit Award for Houses under £300k in 2014 and also won the Stephen Lawrence Prize and RIBA London Award in 2011. Phil Coffey himself is the recipient of many other awards, including being featured in the Faces of British Architecture exhibition in London in 2011. He also serves as a judge at many different architectural awards, highlighting his expertise as an architect. The same culture has seen us submit many different works for competitions and exhibitions. We've handled unique projects such as a Lunar Hotel for Virgin at the Guerrilla Tactics Conference 2011 and a conceptual future bookshop featured in Intelligent Life for their May/June 2014 issue. This quality makes us one of the most trusted architectural firms in the capital, and we are regularly trusted to work on many varied projects not only in London, but throughout the world. As we are a RIBA Chartered Practice, you can be assured that we are the team to rely on for true excellence.
