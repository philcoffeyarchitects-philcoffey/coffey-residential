There is no denying that clean, elegant, and contemporary design continues to be as popular as ever all over the world. These days, the majority of new constructions need not only to be highly functional, but also designed to fit in with their surroundings and harmonise well with the environment, without causing any negative effects. In order to find this unique balance many architects must have the ability to take a step outside conventional thought and use their knowledge and skills in order to find the perfect design. With the rapid growth of population of cities, space is highly sought and thus needs to be used as efficiently as possible in order to make the best out of the area. Offering a range of architectrual services and working with public, commercial and residential architecture and all over the world, we have excelled in the design and completion of a number of structures for a variety of purposes. Our portfolio contains everything from new builds, renovations, and unique extensions for residential clients, to highly functional commercial and public spaces in the UK as well as abroad. Contemporary design is never out of place when the surroundings are is taken into consideration. Structures can be seamlessly introduced next to classic architectural gems by not obstructing or distracting the views, but rather enhancing the location. When it comes to interiors, the careful placement of angles on ceilings, along with a creative use of windows can offer both depth and lightness to a structure without overwhelming its users. Buildings do not simply exist on their own; they are part of the general area in which they are erected and context always needs to be carefully considered. As responsible architects, this is a very important matter to keep in mind when producing solutions for our clients.
