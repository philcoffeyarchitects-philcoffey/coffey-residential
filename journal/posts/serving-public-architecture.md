When it comes to the construction of new buildings and the alteration of existing ones, public authorities and businesses need an architect who can provide them with results that are best for everybody. The architects in control of public building projects need to have a good eye for practicality and ensure that they work that they carry out works beautifully with the surrounding environment, creating architecture that everybody can use. We are highly experienced architects specialising in public, commercial and residential architecture and throughout the world. We have a great deal of experience in a number of different public projects, covering a range of different buildings. We worked on the BFI Reuben Library, opening the library up to appeal to new and casual visitors, and on the entrance to Queen's College, Oxford, creating a beautiful, open space that is well suited to welcoming all visitors. We are also currently working on London's Science Museum, hoping to make it more welcoming and attractive to visitors from all walks of life. We have received numerous awards for our work, including the Retrofit Awards 2014, the Stephen Lawrence Prize and the RIBA London Awards 2011, to name just a few. We're ready to take on various different project types, providing staff that are talented, ambitious and take great pride in the work they carry out. A culture of excellence drives us and we take an empathetic approach to every project, creating architecture which improves the physical, social and environmental context between all buildings involved. As a RIBA chartered practice, you can be assured of our complete dedication to every aspect of architecture. All the work we do is to the benefit of our clients. We will ensure that all parameters are adhered to for the public project and that all budgets are used creatively to make the most of the available resources.
