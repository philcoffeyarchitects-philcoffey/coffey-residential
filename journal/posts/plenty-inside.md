Designing new buildings is not the easiest of tasks. After all, everyone wants to have their own little spin on things. Fortunately, we are a company that excels in this particular area.

![Residential architecture London](images/journal/Canyon-House-Coffey-Architects-300x221.png)

There is a saying that often pops up in our industry. It is that there is a world of difference between a room done by a home decorator and one completed by a professional. Experts like us know all the tricks of the trade. This includes all the most effective interior design tips. By reading on, you can discover some of the tips for yourself.

#### Wood is warming

Firstly, we recommend you warm up spaces using wood. Sometimes, rooms can lack character and warmth. Wood is the ideal antidote here. Timber furniture and accessories are simple ways of adding texture and lifting schemes. However, a 3D wall can be a wonderful asset to spaces lacking focal points. You also don't have to pay ridiculous amounts for this either.

#### A little advice from nature

Another thing you can do is take design advice from nature. There is no need to spend hours looking at pictures of interiors to find your perfect scheme. Have a look around and you will discover inspiration everywhere. When you do, take some photos. Make use of them to aid you in fabricating a concept board. You can then use this to inform your design.

#### Combining styles

There is another useful trick you can use to balance separate styles with the same space and getting the proportions correct. Decorate around 70% of it in a certain way. For the remaining 30%, decorate in a completely different style. This way they can contrast and then create a great look.
