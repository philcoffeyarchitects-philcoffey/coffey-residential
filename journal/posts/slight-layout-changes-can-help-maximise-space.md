We take on projects that vary in size as well as complexity. Our goal is to help clients understand how to make each space as great as possible. Methods of maximising space may appear infeasible and expensive but this need not be the case. There are a number of other ways to make your house feel more spacious. It may only take a few subtle adjustments to make a big difference.

#### A space that feels bigger

Open plan living is a fantastic way to make your home appear more spacious. There are many ways you can achieve this but the easiest one tends to be changing the layout of your home. Don't panic as this is not as invasive as it might sound. You will often find walls in homes that are not necessary so you could easily remove them. This will open up the space without affecting structural stability. Far too often, people have space which they do not utilise and ignore its potential. The most common examples of this are the loft and basement. People think of these as dark and dingy so they will use them solely for storage. What they don't realise is that you can easily carry out a little work and re-purpose the space for a more practical use. This could be a play-room, office, extra bedroom, or any space you want.
