There are many aspects of residential architecture residents need to remember to make the most out of their space. Many people overlook light in relation to their health and property. This does play an important role though, so ensuring good light quality is vital.

#### Natural & Artificial

A lot of people who have a poor amount of natural light in their home make up for it by relying on electrical alternatives. However, this is more costly due to energy usage. In addition to cost, there are studies that show how too much artificial light can be detrimental to vision and cause headaches. This is as well as it interrupting how the body produces melatonin, a hormone that is important for your sleep schedule. As a result it means that artificial light can prevent you from getting the rest you need.

#### How To Get More Natural Light

A building project such as a new build or improvement can be a big benefit. It is a great time to consider how you can get more natural light into the space. Large windows and roof lights are widely available and offer plenty of choice. Bi-folding doors are another great option and let in an abundance of light. In addition, they open up your space to the outdoors. Even smaller touches can make a huge difference. An example is using the right window dressings so you won't obstruct the light. This is as well as considering the placement of taller furniture that might block it. You can also incorporate mirrors and use strategic placement. This will reflect light for a better effect that can also make your space appear bigger.

#### Keep in mind

Research has shown how light can impact how we feel. A lack of light can lead to low mood and motivation. Therefore, you need as much daylight as possible in your living space. The colour scheme within your property needs the right level of light. This will create the best atmosphere and allow you to enjoy the shades you chose. You can also display your home and decor in the best way possible. Another thing light can help with is adding a boost to finishing touches and features in your home. This might be through illuminating statement wall art. You will be able to achieve results for your space that match your needs and tastes.
