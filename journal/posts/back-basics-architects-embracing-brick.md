From glass and steel to timber and concrete, the choice of materials you have for architectural projects is brilliant. But one that is making a strong return is brick. As a part of this, we are happy to work with many ideas to produce a design everyone will enjoy.**A Classic Given New Life**Everyone has a comfort from their past that was always around. For many architects, that is brick. Over the past few decades though, there has been a surge in the popularity of glass and steel buildings. Now people are coming back around and are using brick in modern designs. This is being seen in various forms of residential housing, whether an average home or a high-rise. This type of architecture has always had a large presence within the UK. While popularity in terms of using it in buildings dwindled over the last decade, it is on the rise once again. It fits in with the majority of surroundings and is a great building material. After all, it is beneficial in terms of energy efficiency and is also non-combustible.**A Great Choice**Many people think this surge in demand may have been from people tightening their belts. Regardless, people have rediscovered the amazing aesthetics they can achieve with brick structures. It doesn't appear that the material will ever disappear. With a wide choice of bricks, including different styles and colours, you can give every surface your own touch. One thing that is always impressive is how different types of brick can create an unique feature. By combining brick shapes, colours, and even textures you can present something really special. Whether your project is large or small, we would love to be a part of it. On top of this we get to be a friendly face that offers support throughout your project.
