If there's one team you can trust to handle an architectural project, it's Coffey Architects. Consequently, no matter how challenging something might seem, we allow nothing to stop us in our tracks. No undertaking in our industry is complete without a brief. However, those unfamiliar with our line of work may not know how to produce one. Therefore, we've come up with some expert advice on creating one.**Get the details**If your plans include working on a current build, you'll need details on it. This is so that you and your architect can make some preliminary outlines. You may be able to collect the majority of this information from an estate agent, including interior and exterior photos, and floor plans. With all this at your disposal, you can simply draw over the present blueprints to identify the alterations you want to make.**Grab those photos**You should also aim to include a series of pictures. Using these images, we can discover more about your personal tastes. There's no such thing as too many photos. Ones that focus on your favourite features are the best. Initially, you might not have a final opinion on something. Nevertheless, it's common for people to determine the entire exterior style of their house and then brood over whether an element should be decorative or discrete. These include modern terrace, mews, and detached properties. We can assist you with other endeavours as well. We assure you we will create a brief that is detailed and easy to understand.
