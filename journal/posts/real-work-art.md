With residential architecture, one detail that attracts people to it is the variety. There are many homes in the city with an unique style. A number of them come from the minds of their owners so they stand out from all others. Our own clients have the chance to use their imagination to create [the right design](coffeyarchitects.com/project/). What we do is use our skills to bring their ideas from mind to reality. There is more to architecture than the materials that make the building. Your structure has the potential to be a true work of art. The design can uplift other people and also inspire them. Architectural design has the potential to unite functionality and creativity to influence our moods.

#### What to think about

There are several areas of architecture you can think about to make a project into art. Environmental quality is one such area. Structures with enough outdoor access, ventilation, and natural light are key. This is because they are capable of giving inhabitants a health boost. There are also natural systems. Safe paints, carpets, and furniture can reduce stress levels too. With open, clutter-free spaces and outdoor access, you can really get moving.

#### Sensory environments

Another area you may want to look into is sensory environments. Architecture is capable of exciting or calming its guests. The shapes, lighting, and tactile touches can accomplish this. So can the building's smell and the sounds of your surroundings.

#### Social connections

Finally, there are the social connections. Having that feeling of connection can massively impact your emotions. To draw people together, you can try including several open spaces. This can change the level of community and comfort.
