The best way to get the home of your dreams is to build it yourself. That way you can design each space to suit your needs, use the materials you want, and introduce all the features you need. Coffey Architects has a lot of [experience here](coffeyarchitects.com/project/). Here are some of our top tips if you are taking on this project.

#### Think about the time

Self builds can take a lot of time to finish. You'll need to work on the design, get planning permission, build, and then finish everything off. There could be several delays at each stage. Be realistic with the timeframe and be patient if you need to wait at each stage.

#### Hire professionals

It is always good to have experienced people around you for your project. Start by finding the right architect. They will be a real help when it comes to the design and getting planning permission. The ideas they offer can add a lot of value to your project. In addition, they can help you to avoid a lot of issues. As well as an architect you should hire professional builders and tradespeople. You want the highest quality services possible; never compromise or it can result in a lot of issues. Make sure you check the credentials of everyone you will work with to ensure they are true professionals.

#### Furniture layout

You will probably spend a lot of time with your architect designing spaces. While doing this it is a good idea to think about how you will place your furniture. What you want to do is ensure it fits the space properly. You can also see where you will need to get custom items to suit the dimensions of the room.

#### Other tips

There are plenty of other things we advise our clients to think about during projects. For example we encourage them to plan for mistakes but be ready to adapt in case there are things they can't overcome. We also recommend good communication between everyone involved.
