One of the many ways you can step onto the property ladder is with a self build home. The popularity of this is increasing as you can get your home in a great location. At the end, you can be proud of what you have created. That is why so many people rely on Coffey Architects.

#### Work Out Your Budget

Any project of this magnitude needs a budget. Failing to do this can leave you unable to enjoy your new home because you are worrying about money. There should be a 10% contingency in case of unforeseen problems and costs.

#### Where Will You Build?

You can find land to build on after you have worked out your budget. Having an idea of how your home will look helps you to find a plot that will match. There are many ways that you can find land to build on across London. This includes going to auctions.

#### Consult An Architect

You need a detailed design if you are going to build a home. This requires you to call in a professional architect as well as a structural engineer. Doing this will make sure that you don't forget any essential aspects. Instead, you will end up with a home that you adore.

#### What Is The Timescale?

This type of work calls for a realistic timescale. This will have to factor in the individual aspects of your specific project.

#### Look For Contractors

With a project like this you might get involved with the building, or contract it to professionals. It is surprising how many people you may need to involve. For example you will likely need an electrician, a plumber and builders. You should look into people before you choose to work with them so that you can know that they are reliable.

#### A Schedule of Work

Building your own home is something that can be amazing but there is always the chance of complications. There are problems that can occur which is why you need to make sure that you commit yourself. One way to help with issues that might arise is to come up with a schedule for the project. Taking our advice on-board can help make sure that the project is something you enjoy.
