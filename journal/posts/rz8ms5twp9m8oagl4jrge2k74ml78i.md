One of the most exciting ventures is designing a home. There are limitless possibilities to craft a space that's functional and beautiful. However, there are a few common pitfalls capable of detracting from the final result. Avoiding the mistakes we'll be discussing here can make a substantial difference, especially if you are designing residential architecture.

#### Overdoing it

Firstly, we have over-designing or overloading the space. A common error in home design is the habit of going too far. It could be tempting to include each finish and lovely piece available. Unfortunately, too many elements can create an overwhelming, cluttered space.

is about quality rather than quantity. Concentrate on choosing some stand-out pieces that define the character and style of the room. This approach improves the space's elegance and therefore lets you appreciate each piece.

#### Functionality over aesthetics

The second mistake is neglecting functionality for the sake of aesthetics. In a home design, it's not hard to give too much attention to looks. Yet, you should never sacrifice function. A truly house is one where practicality and beauty come together.

Maybe it's a kitchen that lacks enough storage. Or, it could be a living room with awkward seating arrangements. Whatever the case, neglecting functionality can lead to everyday living being less enjoyable. It ruins the feel of a property, so it won't ever be truly.

#### Too trendy

Following trends too closely can also cost you. The latest trend can entice you, particularly with design. However, new styles and materials tend to emerge frequently. Primarily designing a house based only on recent trends can quickly date the space. You'll reduce its timeless appeal. Something that's trendy today could feel out of date tomorrow.

#### Light it up

Finally, you don't want to ignore the importance of lighting. It is one of the most essential but overlooked aspects of home design. Bad lighting can diminish the impact of even the best designed spaces. On the other hand, the right light can improve each feature and produce ambience.
