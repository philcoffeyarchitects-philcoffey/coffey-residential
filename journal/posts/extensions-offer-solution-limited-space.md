If you are in need of extra living space, you may be considering moving to a new property. With our services for residential architecture and beyond, the layout of your home and available space can be altered and enhanced to meet the needs of your lifestyle. We provide a collaborative service that enables our clients to design and develop homes that facilitate the way they function daily. Contemporary extensions offer the ideal solution to every property, as long as there is enough exterior room to add one. When you engage our team for a residential project we will ensure you are assigned the right individuals for your needs. We listen to our clients and we make sure that we are clear on their ideas. We are fully equipped to complement and enhance your existing home with a modern and simple solution designed to chime perfectly with the surrounding environment. We recently completed work on a side extension on a London terraced property. The aim of the project was to expand the house without the use of structural columns or ostentatious features. The extension offers the simple structural solution of a continuation of a party wall. This delivers the additional space required and features a pitched roof which does not impact the views and functionality of neighbouring properties. Our innovative approach to our work enables us to locate the architectural solutions that are always present.
