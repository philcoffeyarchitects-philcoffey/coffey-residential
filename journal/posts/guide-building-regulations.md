#### What Are They?

These regulations include standards for the design and construction of building work. They apply to new builds and the alterations of existing buildings with a few exceptions. As a general rule, structural work requires approval. The regulations have a lot of detail. They also consider various aspects of the work like materials and the level of workmanship. This is along with fire safety, structural requirements, and energy conservation. Following the regulations helps ensure the quality and safety of the final work. If you fail to follow them you will not get approval. Consequently, this can result in big fines.

#### How Do They Differ From Planning Permission?

Building regulations look in detail at the work, including the construction and choice of materials. The purpose of planning permission is to guide how and where buildings are developed. It takes into account the appearance of the work and how it will impact the environment. This includes how people use the building and highway access.
