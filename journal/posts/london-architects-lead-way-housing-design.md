Architects today are faced with the challenge of constructing homes that meet the needs of a diverse population. Young and lower-paid workers in London are facing a housing crisis, and renting looks set to be the future for the majority. There is a crucial need to create residential developments that will evoke a sense of community and avoid becoming disconnected and insular. We understand that a residential development's success is dependent on its residents. It is important for the future of the city that housing is created for every level of income. London needs to retain its inhabitants while paving the way for new settlers. The major benefit of the conditions of the capital is that they are perfect for dynamic and unprecedented architecture. There have been various factors that have contributed to the contemporary architecture of London being at the forefront of residential design. These include the recovery of the city's economy and a strongly focused local government policy. Furthermore, an internationally motivated design community and a thriving and competitive marketplace make the city ideal for the construction of modern and revolutionary architecture. The future of London's housing also lies with technology. Digital advancement will enable home management from any location and smart materials will contribute to the production of intuitive internal spaces. We specialise in commercial and residential architecture and beyond, creating spaces that accommodate a multitude of personal and professional lifestyles. We take a creative and original approach to every aspect of our work and we are committed to designing and creating structures that perfectly integrate people and places. Fundamentally, people need buildings in which to reside, work and enjoy recreation, and it is our role to create the buildings that positively influence and facilitate the lives of their occupants. We collaborate closely with our clients to ensure a final design that will successfully meet every requirement for practicality and comfort. We assess the precise needs of every project and we assemble the right team for each particular task.
