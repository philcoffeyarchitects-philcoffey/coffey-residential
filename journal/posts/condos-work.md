You may currently be after a [new type of home](http://coffeyarchitects.com/project/#filter=.homes). Prior to deciding on the one you desire however, there are several considerations to make. One potential option would be a condo. Some of you may have heard of this term before. However, others will have no idea of what it is like to live in one.

#### The style

When some people hear the word condo, apartment-style living comes to mind. You buy a single property within a much larger building. As a result, everything existing out of your unit will be owned by another entity. This includes public stairwells and grounds. Luckily, these spaces are generally free for community use.

#### Condos can be apartments and homes

Of course, we must mention that not every condo is an apartment. There are those that are houses within bigger residential structures. No matter what the type of unit is however, the principle remains the same. You own your unit and have exclusive rights to it.

#### Responsibility

Right now, you are probably wondering who holds responsibility for preserving the land outside. The same goes for the elevators and hallways. Like with apartments you may need to pay a service charge for managing them.
