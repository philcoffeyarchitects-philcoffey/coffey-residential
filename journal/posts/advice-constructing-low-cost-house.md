Clients ask us to take part in a wide variety of projects, each of which comes with its own challenges. We have years of experience in overcoming these obstacles. People love the strategies we come up with to succeed in our endeavours. These days, there are many people who would prefer to construct their own home. One of the main reasons for this is that getting onto the housing ladder is becoming more expensive. However, there are plenty of misconceptions out there relating to self-building. Believing them may result in you spending far more than you originally set out. If you wish to build a low cost house, then you should listen to our advice.

#### Keep the geometry simple

A great opening move would be to keep your geometry simple. You don't want to begin with a fussy plan that has complexities and curves. This will certainly add extra costs to your build. Labour is going to account for a significant chunk of the costs here. Therefore, it'll be worth your time examining every detail of your design. For each junction or detail, it would be wise to think about how much things cost.

#### Floor plans

You'll have to be efficient with your floor area as well. Square footage is one of the major contributors to your self build's costs. It would be smart to spend time finding clever ways of lowering the footprint whilst still being able to meet your goals. We can assist you with this. At Coffey Architects, we never lose sight of your budget requirements or any of your other needs.
