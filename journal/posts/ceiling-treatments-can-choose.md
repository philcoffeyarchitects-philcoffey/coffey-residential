If you want to make the most of your decor, you can't overlook anything. You must give consideration for every element, including the ceilings. Their colour, style, shape, and height can massively influence the space's overall mood. However, we are not simply talking about paint vs wallpaper here. Many of the more incredible ceiling treatments originate from creative architecture. Thus, you have to consider every option for your project. We are going to discuss several ceiling types right here to give you a little help.

#### Cove ceilings

It is typical to see cove ceilings inside charming little nooks. Think in an attic corner or under the stairs. They come in all kinds of forms. In addition, they are able to last for a long time in the likes of bedrooms, living rooms, and more. The primary idea here is that your wall will transition into your ceiling at a wide angle.

#### Barrel-vaulted ceiling

Speaking broadly, these are essentially elevated ceilings. They are able to have several nooks carved out so you have lots of design choices. Alternatively, they can be more of a general sloping A-frame design. For a more elaborate round creation, you can consider using a full dome.

#### Ceiling tray

These are merely recessed ceilings where the highest point, which is the centre, gets inverted. If you have high ceilings, they will be able to supply the room with extra depth, making it seem more spacious. The design might be simple, but it will offer you a great finishing touch.
