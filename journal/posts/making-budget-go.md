You don't always have to blow the budget to hire an architect to improve and update your home. When you choose a skilled and experienced architectural practice, you will be working with people who know how to make the most of even a small budget. By making economical and practical decisions in terms of materials and design, architects can make a huge impact on a home without having to spend a fortune. This is what we do when we are hired to update, improve or extend an existing property. Choosing your architect is a crucial part of the process in achieving your vision for your home, and as there are usually significant amounts of time and money involved in these types of projects, it is important that you make the right choice. To begin with, we will be happy to arrange a consultation where we can discuss your ideas and visualise our concepts using a range of different techniques. Once the designs have been created, certain permissions may be required for the construction or alteration work to go ahead. Obtaining planning permission can seem daunting and complex, but we make the process easy. We regularly work with local councils throughout Central London and will take on the task of obtaining the necessary permissions for your project. Once this has been gained and the work begins, we will oversee the entire project and help to co-ordinate the work. If you want to modify your home to make it more comfortable, beautiful and functional, working with a professional architect will ensure that your visions are brought to life while also taking the surrounding environment, timing, budget and materials into careful consideration.
