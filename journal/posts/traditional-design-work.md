We are a company that aims to help its clients to create the best buildings. Our people can work on [all sorts of projects](coffeyarchitects.com/project/), regardless of size. We have experience in both classic and contemporary designs too. Many people shy away from traditional design ideas. They often see them as stuffy, predictable, and boring. However, you should know that many of the facets can be very interesting. Because it makes use of numerous antiques, this type of design enables you to discover some cool pieces. Of course, you have to ask what makes something traditional. We can offer some useful advice on this topic.

#### Order and harmony

To begin with, traditional design aims for a balance of these two elements. It is true that the orderly aesthetic here can seem staid and boring. However, the predictability is there for a reason. Consistency is great because it wants to produce an orderly, calm space. Therefore, symmetry is common, the furnishings will match, and items just appear to work together. Chaotic and wild don't mix with this kind of design.

#### Colour palette

In terms of the colours, warm, dark tones are what tend to define it. With traditional design, you would normally find neutral walls, but colour does have its place. Decor, textiles, and art accents can help a room to stand out. Furthermore, rich, dark jewel tones are a good thing to add.

#### Homely

Finally, for all the predictability, traditional designs will feel like home. Industrial styles and minimalism are often instant wins if your goal is to feel trendy, fresh, and cool. However, for the homey, classic feel, comfortable decor is the right choice. Together with the feeling of predictability comes accessibility and familiarity.
