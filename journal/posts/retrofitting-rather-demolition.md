At Coffey Architects we always strive to address and tackle the main issues in our industry. This ensures we offer projects that provide as many benefits as possible. In addition we create commercial and public buildings that make a great impression.

#### Demolition issues

![Residential architecture London](images/journal/Demolition-300x196.png)

One of the main concerns at the moment is climate change and how much construction contributes to it. There is a focus on creating more sustainable homes and using materials that are greener. However, the fact is that demolishing buildings is very polluting. So, in the process of trying to improve things it actually produces even more harmful emissions. There are many reasons why demolition is so bad for the environment. Firstly heavy machinery consumes a lot of fuel. That means the equipment to bring down properties produces a lot of emissions. In addition there is the cost to transport waste materials and dispose of them.

#### Retrofitting

A better option would be to avoid demolition altogether. It is far better to redevelop a building than it is to start again. While it might be more challenging, it will use fewer resources. It can also help to preserve the heritage of buildings. There are lots of fantastic examples of retrofitting. This includes things like turning old warehouses into new homes and a change of use for old offices and shops. With good designs it is possible to reinvigorate most old building as long as the core is still stable. A deep retrofit can change a host of things, from floor layouts to openings like doors and windows.
