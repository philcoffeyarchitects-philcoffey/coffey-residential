A lot of your attention will be on the interior, but it's vital to think about exterior components too. They will have an impact on the aesthetics and how the property fits into the wider context. Additionally, they affect comfort and your ongoing costs.

#### Siding or cladding

For a long time the favoured design choice in the UK has been to leave bricks unfinished. Even things like pebble-dashing or rendering were only really popular for a short time. However, there has been consistent growing demand for siding and cladding. It gives more control over the aesthetics of the property and can also provide protection against the weather.

One is the ability to resistant the conditions. The last thing you want is to choose materials that will warp and crack. Think about dirt, mildew, and moisture too; they can damage the materials and lead to problems like damp.

#### Doors and windows

The right doors and windows have a big impact on your property because they affect the aesthetics, energy performance, safety, and daily life. Make the wrong choices and you could have issues like higher energy bills, break ins, and high repair costs.

With doors, make sure they can withstand weather exposure and day to day use. Think about the quality of the material, seals, and the components, especially the hinges and locks. The most important thing with windows is to ensure they have good quality seals. Plus, ensure they allow ventilation to prevent condensation and moisture causing issues.

#### Vents and openings

While the doors and windows are the main openings to think about, you can't forget all the little ones. Your home will need openings for various things, including showers, dryers, ventilation, and the stove/hob. It's important to think about what you are using here; ensure you protect the openings and don't allow water and pests to get into the property.

#### Roofing and gutters

The roof and guttering systems are vital for homes. Get it wrong here and you could have water spilling into your home or damaging the foundations. There is a lot to look at, including the style of roofing as well as pitch. Choose materials carefully to ensure they are durable and reliable. Plus, think about roof edges, gutters, and downspouts.
