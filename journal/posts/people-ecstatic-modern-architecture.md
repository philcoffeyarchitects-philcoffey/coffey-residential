When people want to create new homes that offer everything they dream of, they can call on us. Coffey Architects is a firm with plenty of experience in the residential sector. We have a creative outlook that is perfect for designing any kind of home. One form of architecture that people love is the modern variety. It has a little something for everyone. Even if you are more into classical, rustic designs, there are newer design elements that will likely still have appeal. In this post, we are going to dive a little deeper into the style and find out what makes it so popular.

#### Uniqueness

For one thing, modern architecture is unique. There may be simplistic styling that circumvents the additional fluff. However, this style remains distinct. In truth, it has led to some of the most impressive designs in the world today. Some designs might come with a boxy style and flat roofs. Yet, they definitely manage to avoid being boring. If you look around your neighbourhoods and cities, you will probably find that the modern, designs stand out the most.

#### Gentle palettes

Modern architecture also has a restrained palette. Less is certainly more when you are designing a new house. Why would anyone wish to hide those outstanding architectural details of their design with unnecessary ornamentation? The original purpose of these designs was to speak for themselves. They need to make their own statement via their functionality and structure.

#### Warmer

Finally, there is a sense of warmth in modern architecture. One misconception some critics have is that it is cold and cool. Most designs come with attributes that are naturally inviting and warm. This includes stone and wood materials. Furthermore, the plans tend to have fewer walls, producing a more open, welcoming living area. People love this kind of layout.
