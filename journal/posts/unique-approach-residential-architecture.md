If we take a look at the evolution of everyday objects and technology like computers, televisions, and mobile phones, we can see that these items have become progressively smaller and much easier to use. This is reflected in the fact that there is an increasing demand for simpler, more elegant architecture and design. While many styles can be found in architecture, lately there has been an increase in the preference for more minimal designs in residential, commercial, and public spaces. This is possibly due to the societal needs of simplifying our lifestyles for convenience, and minimising the amount of material used in an effort to become more environmentally conscious. With the preference of structures moving towards a more simplified look, architects are faced with the challenges of creating depth, functionality, and inspiring creativity with their designs. To achieve this, professional architects in London are increasingly utilising the elements surrounding the location of the property and taking these deep into consideration when creating designs.
