There are many people who have the skills to design buildings. What makes us different however is our approach of thinking outside of the box. The leading remodelling and design platform Houzz recently published its 2022 Summer Survey. It is full of wellness design trends. What this survey did is measure searches on its huge site during 2022's first quarter. It then made comparisons to the same period in 2021. The goal was to see what is popular right now. We want to look at some of these things here.

#### Take your lifestyle outside

Among the searches was a persisting focus on outside living. Hot tubs on decks had the largest gains, with a 130% increase. Such exterior spas supply a number of health benefits. Examples include cardiovascular improvements, pain relief, improvements in sleep, muscle relaxation, and stress relief. Not surprisingly, more people are looking to create them at home so they have somewhere to relax.

#### Task lighting

![Residential architecture London](images/journal/Modern-Side-Extension-Coffey-Architects-300x186.png)

Something else that is a big feature in wellness design trends is task lighting. This is a functional enhancement to many spaces in a home. For example, having suitable illumination on your countertop in the kitchen can make all the difference. This is between a julienned fingertip and a julienned carrot. With more people working from home now too, a living room's or spare bedroom's regular ceiling light fixtures are typically inadequate. Poor lighting set ups can lead to headaches and eye discomfort. You can even expect your capacity to carry out precision work to diminish. So, it is better to upgrade to task lighting.

#### A key piece of furniture

One other functional trend would be hallway cabinets. They have risen 101% in popularity. A nice piece of furniture can aid homeowners in keeping the hall more organised and hygienic. There will also be room for the shoes and bags of inhabitants. Plus, you can add a touch of class and style to the space with the right furnishings.
