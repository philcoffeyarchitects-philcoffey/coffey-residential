The idea of coming to an architect after purchasing a new home might strike you as strange. An architect, in the minds of most, is involved with the design and build of a house. The reason for this is because people are seeing the benefits of buying a suitable property and changing it to meet their needs and wishes instead of holding out in the hope of eventually finding the perfect home. We are highly experienced at dealing with all aspects of residential architecture and our ability goes beyond creating new builds. A mindset many are developing - and one we at Coffey Architects strongly endorse - is to not look for rooms in a property, but rather focus on the space that you need. If a property looks like it is ideally suited to your space requirements but the layout of the rooms is not quite what you wanted, do not turn your back on it if the deal is right. We will empower you to change the layout of your property to become the living space you aspire to having. We can also create extensions to give you extra living space without you having to leave a property you love or face the hassle of moving house. You should also not feel put off by being unsure as to what you would change in your property to improve it. Our belief at Coffey Architects is that architectural design is a highly involved and engaging process. We are here to either inspire or suggest suitable changes to draw up the plans you want just as much as we are here to transform your specific ideas into workable architectural plans. When it comes to London, we are always keenly aware of the need to create modern, practical plans that suit life in the 21st Century, and yet also to work in a way that is completely suited to the existing building and its surrounding environment. Due to our attentive and sympathetic approach, we are often called upon to work with period and listed properties, making them into useful and functional residences while retaining the charm and original features that make them special. If you require help with any aspect of residential architecture, we are your first choice.
