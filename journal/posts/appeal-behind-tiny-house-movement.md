In this age, people spend a lot of their lives accumulating possessions. This can result in a lot of clutter and if you are not careful, spaces will look poor. Cluttered rooms can even have a negative effect on your health. Trying to combat this is the tiny house movement. It is something we are familiar with through [our work](http://coffeyarchitects.com/selection/homes/) on residential architecture. Smaller houses can be very cosy and result in less space for clutter.

#### The big idea behind tiny homes

![Residential architecture London](images/journal/Tiny-House-1-272x300.png)

The "Tiny House" movement uses the idea of minimalism. It focuses on living a simple life in a little space. Inspiration came from the many people who liked what happened when they lowered their expenses through living in a smaller space without as much stuff. They were able to have a lot more free time and found that they were much happier. Residents of tiny houses often say that they are able to declutter their lives. This can help with making important choices when it comes to their living environment. It is also a less expensive way of living that allows for a more sustainable life.

#### Growing popularity

More and more people are searching the internet to learn about tiny homes. There is also far more about it on social media. As a result, the number of people joining the movement and changing their lives is growing. The freedom it offers, amount people can save and the way it helps the environment are all great reasons to get involved. Issues with the availability of space to build new homes also encourage more people to think about smaller, more dynamic spaces.
