It could be because of a slope, access problems, or another issue. However, good designs can often overcome these issues. At Coffey Architects we can help you. Sometimes the most incredible properties come because [the design](coffeyarchitects.com/project/) needs to tackle a very specific problem.

#### Slopes can be beneficial

![Residential architecture London](images/journal/Building-on-a-slope-200x300.png)

While the idea of building a home on a site that slopes may seem impossible, there are ways around it. In fact, the slope could actually be a blessing in disguise. It could leave you with a very unique home that has an incredible orientation. One of the ways to build on a slope is to cut and fill. This process literally requires carving out a level surface. It can be costly to do but work really well. The most important thing to remember here is the excavation must reach the original subsoil bearing to provide stability. Another option is to build the home on stilts or columns. This can be an effective choice and can save the need to create extensive foundations.

#### Drainage

An important thing to think about on sloping sites is drainage. It may work in your favour and allow you to create shallower drainage. However, in some cases you may need a pump and a more complex system to pump water uphill. You also need to be careful with the design to make sure rainwater won't be flooding into the property.
