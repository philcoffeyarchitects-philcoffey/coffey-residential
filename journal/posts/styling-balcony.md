For the lucky individuals that own one, having a quiet morning on the balcony is a simple pleasure. When the summer arrives, you will want to enjoy those weekend al fresco breakfasts and brunches to the fullest. Relaxing evenings can be great too. However, as opposed to your lovingly curated living room or kitchen, balconies can get overlooked in terms of the style. Often, it will look bare and fail to meet its full potential. Regardless of how small the balcony is, there is plenty you can do to make it more functional and stylish. We have some ideas that can help you with this.

#### Lighting

The first idea we will go over is adding some lighting. You may wish to sit outside long into those summer evenings. If so, some lights will be necessary to set the tone. Wind some fairly lights around your railings to produce ambience. Another option would be grouping lanterns into a corner for a subtle glow. If you are lacking in sockets or wiring, battery or solar power options are the way to go.

#### Use greenery

Our next balcony idea is bringing it to life with greenery. There are very few places that can't be enhanced by the inclusion of flowers or leafy plants. Accessorising your railings with pots and hanging planters is a fantastic way of adding texture or colour to smaller balconies. Corner planters and wall racks can aid you in getting the most out of vertical space.

#### Rugs

The final balcony idea we will discuss is laying down a rug. This may not have been your initial thought whilst updating everything. However, using the right one can really transform your exterior space. You will get that outdoor/indoor vibe whilst also making it more comfortable for people to walk. But, remember to put it away if it is going to rain.
