You have to ask yourself what sets the best house designs apart from the other properties in a neighbourhood. It could be decoration, landscaping, or architecture. For the majority of houses, it is a mixture of all these elements. They work together, at least until the sun goes down. At night, you are unable to see any of these details unless they are properly lighted. Exterior lighting is able to add safety to your house and make things more secure. At the same time, it needs to accentuate the distinct features that make your property charming during the day. By following the tips we have for exterior lighting, you can make the most of it.

#### Focus on distinct elements

To begin with, you should concentrate on distinct architectural elements. Most people light pathways from the driveway to the front door. But, not enough of us focus on those features that can produce a dramatic view of our houses. You are missing a big opportunity to enhance residential architecture here. One thing you can do is wash the walls by pointing a wide light beam at it from a few feet away. Task lights are necessary for this. But you will make your home look unique with accent lighting.

#### Matching

You will also want to make sure that everything matches. Is yours a modern or more traditional house? Your lighting style should match the property to pull every detail together. Avoid using an abstract, modern wall sconce for a traditional building. Likewise, don't use a lantern pendant light on a modern house. What you need to do is select styles that work well with other elements. Match materials or colours, or find appropriate complements. By doing this, you can shine lights on what is beautiful already and add to it.
