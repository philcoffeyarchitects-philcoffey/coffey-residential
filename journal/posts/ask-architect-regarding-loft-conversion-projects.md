We are more than happy to work with clients who have ideas that range in size and level of complexity. There are a few different things to consider if you are going to invest in a loft conversion. For example, this could require you to make a noticeable change to your property's architectural design. Because of this you should consult with an architect and hire their services for the project. There are also several questions you should ask them about the work.

#### Can I convert my loft?

Some lofts will be more suitable for conversion than others. You need to look at things like your home's architectural design to find out about your options. You can have a professional survey your [home](http://coffeyarchitects.com/project/#filter=.homes) to learn if it is suitable to convert the loft.

#### Do I need planning permission?

Chances are you won't need planning permission to convert your loft. You will, however, need this if the conversion will extend or alter the roof-line. Your architect will be able to help you establish whether it is necessary for your project. Regardless of whether you need planning permission, you will need to comply with fire safety codes and building regulations.

#### Why should I work with you?

You need to know that your home is in good hands and you are dealing with a professional. You can trust the information your architect provides when they have the right level of training and plenty of experience. Look at examples of previous work so that you can see the quality of their work.

#### How long will the work take?

The lead time of a project can vary and depends on numerous factors. This can include the time to draw up plans, availability of materials, and how long it takes to get permission if it is necessary. Once your architect has a better idea of the details, they will be able to provide you with a general idea. It is important to be flexible as even with the best planning, delays can occur.

#### How much will the work cost?

There are a huge number of variables that affect the price of a loft conversion. The choice of materials, number of workers, and the type of property will all influence the cost. When you discuss your ideas with an architect, they should be able to provide you with a ballpark figure. Speak to them about your budget early on so that they can help you stay within it.
