When it comes to residential architecture and throughout the world, our team provide the best possible service regardless of the size or nature of the project. We believe that people should be perfectly comfortable within their homes, and that the spaces that they reside in should connect to their surrounding environment whilst being as functional as possible. When you work with our team, you can be assured that we will provide you with the full extent of our expertise and strive to design the perfect residence for you. We are inspired and driven by a culture of excellence that resonates throughout our practice. With various teams for various project types, you can be assured that you will receive a team specialising in residential architecture and receive a completely focused service, whether you are having a renovation, extension, or even building an entirely new residence. Once a design has been agreed on, we will work extensively with you in order to ensure that the ensuing project is carried out to the highest standards, on budget and on time, using the best possible materials and executed by teams of highly trained professionals. We have a vast portfolio of projects that our team has helped to design and see through to completion. We have completed projects involving new builds, such as the light house, a house overlooking Cork Harbour in Ireland. We have also performed extensions, such as our modern side extension without the need for columns or extensive infill materials, using brick, glass and aluminium to create a continuation of the party wall without compromising on space or impacting on neighbouring properties. We have worked on countless renovations, transforming and connecting units to create open, flowing spaces, as well as renovating a two storey apartment to reinvent it as an urban loft with a modern design constructed from sleek materials. We've also renovated two warehouse apartments to connect them together with one continuous space. We gladly undertake all types of residential architecture and throughout the UK and you can be fully assured that our clients are our primary concern. Our team work on all residential projects from new homes to extensions, and offer everything from planning applications to project management, ensuring that your project is executed perfectly and that all work is carried out to the highest possible standards.
