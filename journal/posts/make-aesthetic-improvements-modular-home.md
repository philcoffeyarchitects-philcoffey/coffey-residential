With residential architecture, London residents want something that reflects their personality. They need a design to help them to stand out from the crowd. Our firm has years of experience [helping people with this](coffeyarchitects.com/project/).

![Residential architecture London](images/journal/computer-desk-desktop-2811648-300x200.jpg)

Our houses are where we spend most of our time outside of work. In recent times however, different working arrangements have led to our homes becoming our work spaces. Therefore, it makes sense to make them cosy and nice. This is for aesthetic reasons as well as functional ones. With a pleasant workplace, you will end up with a more productive work output. Moreover, having an appealing setting never hurts. If you are planning on enhancing a modular home, the following advice will help.

#### The right design

To begin with, you will need to pick the right modular house. Take note of features, location, size, and budget. With the right details, you will have an easier time planning the improvements for your home. We recommend beginning small with modular dwellings. Usually, these are easier to upgrade and decorate. The available space you will have shall be just right. There won't be any need for you to worry about having too many things inside.

#### The facade

You will also have to design the facade. This is one of the more entertaining parts of updating modular houses. You can begin with your doors and windows. We suggest matching materials and colours here. Uniformity is pleasing for the eyes. You also have the choice to add claddings or wall designs. This shall make a shade of contemporary design and enhance your walls outside. You can have a small garden to improve the house too. Such a move can amplify the environment and ambience around the vicinity. It gives people nice things to look at while they are working too.
