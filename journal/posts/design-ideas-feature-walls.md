Feature walls are an excellent way to accent your space and add more style to the property. But, there are certain common mistakes people make whilst designing these walls. What we are going to do here is examine some ideas for the feature walls. We will go over what you should and shouldn't do, but if you still need help you can [come to us](https://www.coffeyarchitects.com/projects).

#### Painting

![Residential architecture London](images/journal/Bold-paint-227x300.jpg)

Firstly, you must paint correctly. There is nothing worse than a room that's poorly painted. It will be even more obvious if you do a poor job with a feature wall because it is meant to stand out and get attention. You don't want people looking at it and thinking it hasn't been done professionally. Ensure you prepare your equipment and walls prior to starting. Most importantly, make sure the surface is smooth. Cover any electric sockets and switches with tape too. Covering your furniture and floors with sheeting will also aid you in keeping them safe from paint splashes. Whilst painting, make use of a 'M' or 'W' pattern to keep the colour even.

#### Go natural

Second, you should choose an obvious wall. Creating feature walls should feel natural. Don't go selecting them at random. Instead, opt for the one that naturally works as the focal point of each room. If you fail to do that, everything will look odd, giving the room an unnatural feel.

#### Be bold

It is always a good idea to use bold block colours on your feature wall. It should be attractive and provide the room with a wow factor. Be careful however if you're utilising neutral colours for the remainder of the room. Ensure the bold colour you go for complements them. Normally, if you select a bold shade, there will be a recommended colour palette related to it. This will help you find the ideal shades.

#### Alternating

Finally, use alternate materials for the feature walls. Going for things like stone and tiles can bring more interest to the space. It will become a talking point. Something else this approach will do is bring a bit of the outside inside. You can also choose things like different moulding and accents to add more unique elements.
