One detail you may want to consider when building a new home is a security gate. Regardless of whether you live in a low or high crime area, having one makes a lot of sense. They function as the first line of defence against would-be burglars and intruders. In many cases, a gate will be sufficient to deter criminals completely. However, they can restrict the view of the public too. This makes it more troublesome for thieves to get a long-distance view. The question therefore is how to choose the ideal security gate for your driveway and house. In this post, we will be discussing a number of potential options.

#### A choice of styles

There are actually a number of gate styles available. Bi-folding and swing are among the most common ones. Every security gate will demand its own specs. Thus, the right one for your property shall depend on multiple factors, such as space and the level of security you need. With swing gates, these are the most traditional type of security gate. They are divided into two halves. These separate and swing backwards when you need to open them. Because of the swinging movement, the gate demands a large area so they can open fully. As for bi-fold gates, they are similar to swing ones. They just don't have the space demands. When these gates open, the gate leaf folds in half in the middle. It needs roughly half the space of a swing one to completely open. The gates can open and close rapidly. Therefore, they are better for high traffic locales. With both types of gate you can choose how they operate too. For example you can have manual gates you need to open by hand. Or, there can be electronic ones. They are powered by multiple motors, arms, and rams so you can control them with the touch of a button or a keyfob.

#### Driveway layout

Your driveway's layout will also have a major impact on the kind of gate you can install. By the nature of their design, specific gates require much wider openings. An expert will listen to your requirements, inspect the space, and decide on the most appropriate option.
