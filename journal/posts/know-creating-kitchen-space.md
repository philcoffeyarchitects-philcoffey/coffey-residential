#### Don't Overlook It

With any house, a kitchen is a space that needs planning with care. The design should think about how people will use the space, not only for food preparation but also eating and entertaining guests. You need it to flow with the rest of the layout so you should think about it towards the beginning of the design process. There are different areas of a kitchen that need to work together. There needs to be a sink area, preparation space, room for cooking as well as storage for food, appliances and utensils. A client may specify other things that they want in their kitchen such as a dining area connecting to it. You will want to think about how they will go together and how people will move around the space for efficiency.

#### Fitting together

With things like cupboards and appliances, you will realise that they need to fit together. If they do not, people can have trouble making the design a reality, especially with furniture. Consider all aspects against everything else to help make it easier. In addition factor in elements such as electrics and plumbing. The materials will also have to suit the setting. Some are wiser for kitchens due to durability and ease of cleaning. For example stainless steel is a popular option. Some people opt for an approach where the design makes it easy to change the appearance. This prolongs its life because you can change the doors and not the structure for a new look and a minimal cost.
