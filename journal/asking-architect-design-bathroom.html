Asking an architect to design your bathroom

Words · Coffey Residential · 1 min read

There are a few crucial things you need to get right when you design your ideal home. One of them is ensuring you get the bathroom right. It is a space that needs to be functional, clean, and comfortable. You may even want to ask your architect for help to design it.

Why work with an architect?

The thing you need to keep in mind is that architects see the big picture. As a result they can handle the design, project management, and much more. You may think a bathroom is simply picking elements you like but you need to make sure they work together. You also need to think about light, ventilation, heating, and more. This can help you to avoid issues with the space later. One of the most important things here is choosing the location for the bathroom. The days of it being a late addition right at the back of a home are long gone. Now it needs to be in the right location. For example will it be a family bathroom available to everyone? Or are you thinking of having private en-suites in bedrooms? You need to think about accessibility, convenience, and even things like noise. It is also a good idea to ask an architect about materials. They can offer lots of options and suggestions. For example you may have a specific colour or design in mind but not know exactly what type of tile to choose. The architect can help and consider things like longevity.
