What do I need to make a building sustainable?

Words · Coffey Residential · 1 min read

Our team has been working in this field for years. This means we are more than capable of helping you with your projects. More people are choosing to introduce sustainability to their projects. Many of them are doing this by carefully selecting the materials and being specific with their design. By getting things right, you can produce a robust, healthy home that will last for a long time. The following are some of the main elements in designing a sustainable building.

Consider the mass

You need to use the structure's mass to the best possible effect. Materials like concrete and masonry brick can result in thermal mass inside a building. This can take in the warmth from the sun's rays during the day. When temperatures drop, it releases the heat into your home. As a result the process helps create a comfortable interior setting. Several natural building products can boost thermal mass. Therefore they can assist the structure in moderating the inside environment.

Structural systems

Select your structural systems early on in the design procedure as well. You are able to adapt every construction system in order to ensure excellent degrees of energy efficiency. However, there are those that lend themselves more directly to meeting the highest standards.

Renewable technology

Finally, you should deploy your renewable technologies once you complete your shell design. Don't do so any earlier. There is a golden rule here; you must design your building to carry out the work and match your energy requirements to that level. At Coffey Architects, we can assist you in making homes more sustainable if it is what you want. If there is anything we can do for you, please let the team know.
