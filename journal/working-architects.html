Working with our architects

Words · Coffey Residential · 2 min read

We pride ourselves on designing unique and innovative commercial and residential architecture and throughout the globe. With a plethora of awards and achievements to our name, we complete work on residential, public and commercial projects alike, providing a full service not just across the UK but to a variety of countries around the world. When you choose to work with our team, regardless of the project, you can be assured of excellence. We work across all sectors to ensure that as many customers as possible can take advantage of our architectural expertise. We want to ensure that our services can provide benefit for all kinds of buildings, and have worked on a huge array of projects as a result. We have worked on numerous examples of residential architecture and beyond, making the most of all usable space and giving our customers unique living solutions. In houses with limited space, we can create fitting extensions and can also work on renovations so that our customers can make the most out of the space that they have in their home. We also work on commercial buildings, having provided our unique eye for design in regards to projects in London, China, Bangladesh and further afield. We have also entered a variety of competitions, giving life to a range of unique creations such as the Inside Out House, an unique dolls house in aid of KIDS, and Craftword, an envisioning of the bookshop of the future for the May/June Intelligent Life issue in 2014. Recently, we won the contract to renovate the Research Centre at London's Science Museum, and have provided our expertise with re-designing the entrance to Queen's College in Oxford, providing a design for the Tricycle Theatre in London and office renovations for barristers in Middle Temple, London. We have gathered numerous awards in the past few years, including the 2011 Stephen Lawrence Prize, Small Project Architect award at the Architect of the Year Awards in 2012 and, most recently, the Retrofit Award in 2014 for the category of House Under £300,000. We have a number of other awards and look forward to earning more over the course of 2015. Our dedicated team will ensure that you receive the design that all work carried out is to the highest possible standards, whether you require architects to work on public, commercial or residential architecture.
