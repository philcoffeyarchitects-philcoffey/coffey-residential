Architecture informed by travel

Words · Coffey Residential · 1 min read

As a particularly inventive firm of professionals dealing with public, residential and commercial architecture, we have gained a reputation for our determination to create the most unique structures. We deliver work that is based on our deep belief that the cultures and landscapes we find across the surface of the earth can effectively contribute to the way we design and construct new architecture. 15 years ago, Phil Coffey conceived of the idea to amalgamate his personal passions of travel and photography in order to scour the globe and acquire an unique collection of photographs to inform the work of our practice. It is our belief that the word "outside" represents not that which we can simply see through our windows but the outside that covers the globe. Travelling the world has enabled us to gain valuable insight into the way architecture manages to embed itself into the way we live our lives. Witnessing some of the many different cultures of the world and the presence of the architecture amongst them solidifies our belief that architecture is about buildings possessing empathy for their surroundings and the way we function. Due to our passion for the preservation of culture and progression of learning, we constantly undertake projects that excite us and that will make a valuable contribution to the way we live, learn and embrace culture and education. If you are in search of London-based architects who are capable of producing beautiful buildings which not only look great but are engineered to enhance the way we live, work and play, we will work hard to meet all your requirements and produce work informed by our experience, our travels and our passions.
