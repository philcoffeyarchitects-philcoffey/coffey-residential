Creating the space required for successful loft conversions

Words · Coffey Residential · 2 min read

In years gone by a loft conversion was considered to involve little more than putting down a bit of reinforced flooring down in an attic. The intention in these cases was seldom to use it for anything more than some extra storage space. Whilst this was a perfectly understandable approach to take it is a little outdated. In this day and age of space being at a people want to make more use of every piece of room, including in the loft. One of the aspects of our work that we have always promoted in respect of our clients' thinking is to consider the potential amount of space on offer. This is all the more true in terms of loft conversions. Whilst one might naturally think that the scope for conversion work is limited to the existing area of a loft or attic space, it's often feasible to change this. In many cases there's the margin to either lower the floor or raise the ceiling as part of the conversion project. Making this change can often have a significant impact on achieving success with a project. If you're currently considering having loft conversion work carried out on your property then it would add value to get us involved. We truly excel when it comes to residential architecture and have been behind many highly regarded projects across London. We have a wonderful understanding of space and how to make the right use of it. We're always delighted to be part of a project of this nature, in particular when it's all at a provisional stage. Our knowledge and insights will enable you to make a more considered and informed decision on the way to move forward with the work. We can also offer comprehensive design advice, focusing on your ideas. We provide a comprehensive, start-to-finish set of services in the work we do for you. In taking responsibility for the project management of the residential architecture we deliver we're able to utilise our skills and knowledge in a way that achieves the best results for you. From planning and party wall permissions through to the final work, we're with you at every stage to ensure that perfection is achieved.
