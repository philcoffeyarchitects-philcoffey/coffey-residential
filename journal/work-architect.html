Why work with an architect?

Words · Coffey Residential · 2 min read

One of the main misconceptions that people have when remodelling or building a home is that they do not need to work with an architect. However, it's important to know that even if you are able to create your own design, there are many other things to take into consideration such as construction and infrastructure. An architect is likely to be the most valuable asset you can have when undertaking any kind of building project, so unless your project is extremely simple, it's best to consult a professional before any work commences. Architects are trained and highly skilled in making your visions into a reality and all good firms will make sure that you're involved in the whole process, from planning to construction. Prior to contacting an architect, it's helpful to come up with a brief on your own. This should include a wish-list of what you want and expect from the finished structure. You also need to think about how you will actually use it. Will it be an extra bedroom or an extension to your kitchen? This approach will help your architect to establish a course of action. Budget should be thought about at this stage too, so you can avoid disappointment or high expenses at a later date. By working with professional architects, you can ensure that every aspect of the project goes smoothly, from gaining planning permission to choosing materials to ensuring the project stays within the required budget and deadline. When you are collaborating with a professional architect you will have access to valuable information that you wouldn't have otherwise had, leading to a better project and a final result that will exceed your expectations. If you've never worked with an architect before, it's understandable and likely that you will have a few questions. Our specialist architectural teams are here for you throughout the entire design process making sure that all of your needs are met. Whether you would like a home extension for additional space or have always wanted to built your very own design, we're able to accommodate you. We don't only work on residential architecture, but regularly handle private and commercial projects throughout the entire world.
