What are the most important rooms in a home?

Words · Coffey Residential · 2 min read

Designing your own home is really great because you can get all the features you want. However, you need to take care here. You have to make sure you give enough attention to the most important spaces. Below we will take a look at our picks for some of the most important rooms in any home.

The entrance

A space people often overlook is the entrance. This is a mistake. It is one of the most important rooms in the property for a number of reasons. Firstly it sets the tone and feel for the property. If the entrance is cold and uninviting, people won't feel welcome. From an external standpoint, the entrance can also influence how people view the whole property. If you want to maximise the kerb appeal, you need to get the entrance design right.

The kitchen

Many people call this the heart of the home. It gets this name because it is such a crucial space. A great kitchen can make or break a property. Just think about how many times you will use it every single day. You need to maximise usability with your design, ensuring the space will suit your needs. Then, you also need to make sure it is aesthetically pleasing. The right layout, high quality materials, and the right appliances will make all the difference here.

The master bathroom

While many homes now have en suite bathrooms, the master bathroom is still a key part of the property. It needs to be a space that feels like a spa. At the same time you need to make sure that everyone who walks in is impressed by the design and detail. Feature sinks, beautiful lighting, and relaxing elements are all important here.

The room where you relax

Everyone relaxes in different ways. You may prefer exercising, curling up with a book, or enjoying movies or games. What you need to do is invest time and money to create the perfect room for relaxing. Your home should be somewhere it is easy to unwind. However, you may struggle to relax even in a cosy bedroom or lounge. So, having a space specifically for relaxation is great. Just make sure it can include everything you need.
