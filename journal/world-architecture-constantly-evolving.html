The world of architecture is constantly evolving

Words · Coffey Residential · 2 min read

A key quality of commercial and residential architecture is the dynamic way in which is it seemingly constantly evolving and changing, and yet always gives a sense that everything is where it always has belonged. Designing a new property which blends seamlessly with the city skyline, yet also extols a sense of the vibrancy of the new, is a rare skill. Here at Coffey Architects we have shown time and again in our work to have an excellent ability to create architecture that works within its surroundings and context while remaining fresh, new and unique. Our practice is home to some of the most versatile, visionary and adaptive architects in the city. They all share the creative vision and aspirations of our founder, the Phil Coffey, whilst holding their own specialist skills and abilities. When it comes to the wishes, requirements and ideas you have, either for commercial or residential architecture, in London or further afield, we have the people ready to engage with you to deliver both the plans and the project management you can rely on to see everything completed successfully. Over the years we have become the established name for designing properties - whether they are new builds or renovations - that both meet the style, appearance and functionality you want, yet also resonate with their surroundings beautifully. These inherent abilities have seen our services called upon both throughout the UK and across the globe. We place a strong emphasis on ensuring our planning and projects meet your requirements and limitations. We do not expect you to somehow be able to change your space, circumstance or budgetary requirements to meet what we have in mind. Whether the project you have in mind is the architecture for a loft conversion or for a large public building that reaches into the skies, we work with you to deliver what you want in place. Some have the sense that visiting and using a highly regarded architect can be an intimidating experience. After one meeting with Phil Coffey and the team we would like to think you become aware that this is far from the case. We have a passion for what we do; one that drives us to deliver the most inspirational architectural services available.
