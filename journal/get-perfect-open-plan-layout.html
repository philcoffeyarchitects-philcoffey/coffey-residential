How to get the perfect open plan layout

Words · Coffey Residential · 2 min read

Open plan layouts are probably the most popular style for modern homes. They make rooms feel much bigger and brighter, while also making interiors feel more comfortable. Whether you want this kind of layout for a kitchen, a bedroom, or the whole home, Coffey Architects can help. We design some of the most amazing residential architect London has to offer. It would be our pleasure to help you get the right design too. There are plenty of things you can do to get the right open play layout. Below we will look at some of the best options.

Furniture placement

One thing people tend to do is place their furniture along walls. If you want to open up the space though, something as simple as moving the sofa and dining table away from them can help. Doing this makes it possible to walk all around them, creating more flow in a room.

Cohesion

Many people break up spaces by using different colours in their decor. However, if you are thinking open plan, it may be best to have a cohesive theme throughout. That way the spaces flow together and feel part of a whole rather than separate spaces.

Look at the floor and ceiling

You can create the same sense of cohesion by having the right flooring and using it throughout rooms. It can tie them together effectively, just like having the same colours on the walls. It is a good idea to think about the ceilings too. High ones are great for open plan.

Curves

Round furniture is a great idea here. It can soften the floor plan and creates a more open feel. Angular furniture will usually feel like it should be against the walls and can look quite harsh.

Moveable and multipurpose

It is also a really good idea to choose furniture you can move easily and use for different things. Both of these things mean the space can evolve and adapt to suit various needs. This is a vital part of open plan designs.
