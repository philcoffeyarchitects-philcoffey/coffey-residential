The right ways to enhance residential security

Words · Coffey Residential · 1 min read

For over a decade, we have worked hard to produce some of the most distinct buildings in the world. No matter how demanding a client's requirements are, we take the steps necessary to ensure that they're met. It's during projects like renovations and new builds that homeowners disregard the gravity of security solutions. If introduced during the preliminary phases, they can operate almost as if they were invisible. Additionally, they can coordinate with the submitted blueprints. To give you some ideas of how you can incorporate security, we've come up with a series of tips for you to use. Which areas require the most consideration? Individuals tend to evaluate doors first whenever homeowners contemplate security measures. It's natural to think that the front door will be the main target of intruders. However, they only take this approach when they're certain that there are no witnesses and when they get reckless. One rule to remember with door security is to think about multi-point locking arrangements. Your door locks in a minimum of three locations once you turn the key. This will make them harder to open by force. Something else you should know is that within properties, glass is typically the weakest material. As such, criminals think of glass doors and windows as unprotected entry points. You can't replace certain windows without sizeable applications. But, it is possible to laminate the interiors with a special film. At Coffey Architects, our work within the residential sector has attracted the attention of clients from around the world. To make sure that everything goes swimmingly, we keep in constant contact with our customers. This prevents us from making mistakes and allows us to add new features whenever necessary. If there's something we can do for you, please let us know.
