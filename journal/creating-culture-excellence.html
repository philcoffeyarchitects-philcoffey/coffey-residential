Creating a culture of excellence

Words · Coffey Residential · 1 min read

Architecture is something that evolves over time, never standing still for too long and changing across eras. We create public, commercial and residential architecture, one of the most architecturally diverse cities in the world, as well as working on projects across the globe. We pride ourselves on doing as much as we can to help architecture advance, delivering innovative solutions for all of our customers and taking advantage of new materials and technology as much as possible in our work. We work extensively on residential architecture and beyond for private individuals, whether it is designing extensions or developing entirely new buildings, and we also work extensively in the public sector. We have a lot of experience creating public buildings, having recently secured a contract to work on the new research centre for the Science Museum in London, and have worked on a myriad of different projects from hotels to libraries to schools. We provide a service that covers the entire process. We will help seek planning applications for any and all work and will liaise extensively with local authorities to ensure that planning permission is secured. We will administer building contracts and can also oversee the work. Our customers are involved in all decisions that we make and we keep them constantly updated throughout the entire process. With an array of rewards and accolades, and a huge list of satisfied customers throughout the world, we are dedicated to continuing our culture of excellence. Every job that we take on is completed fully and to the highest possible standards.
