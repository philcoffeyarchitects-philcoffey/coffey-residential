Transforming residential properties

Words · Coffey Residential · 2 min read

Your home should be an extension of your life and an expression of your personality. Likewise, it should be as comfortable and functional as possible, making it easy for you to live, work, relax and play. If you need to give your home a facelift in terms of aesthetics, accessibility or design, and are in search of the ideal architect in London to help you do it, then our professionals are here to help. We pride ourselves on providing fully comprehensive services and working with clients from throughout the UK and even further afield. Our dedicated team of architects have one goal, and that is to ensure that you are provided with results that make your life better. We work closely with you to provide a design that matches your vision as far as possible. Whether it is a refurbishment, an extension or an entirely new property, we will provide you with plenty of creative input and visualise the designs for you so you know what to expect. We want all of our clients to benefit from good architecture and we provide a complete service to give them the property, extension or refurbishment that they desire. We can introduce our own consultants to provide impartial advice and opinions and we can also oversee the project to ensure that it is completed to the highest possible quality. From the actual design to the full construction of the project, all of our clients can benefit from our fully comprehensive service and can be assured that we will be there for them consistently, updating them on the work and meeting all deadlines set. We are helmed by Phil Coffey, a highly talented architect in London who has gained a plethora of awards for his visions and work. From the Stephen Lawrence Prize to a Retrofit Award, we continue to grow and foster a culture of excellence. When it comes to residential architecture, we pride ourselves on doing the job as thoroughly as possible and leaving no stone unturned throughout the process. Every single aspect of the work will be carefully considered. As a RIBA chartered practice, our clients can be assured that we will provide them with nothing less than the highest quality architectural services available.
