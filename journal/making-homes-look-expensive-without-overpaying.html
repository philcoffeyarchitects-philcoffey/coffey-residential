Making homes look expensive without overpaying

Words · Coffey Residential · 2 min read

These days, when someone wants to design a new home, they prefer to be innovative. There are already too many places out there that look the same. As a result, it does not make much sense to add to them. Plus, would one of these standard designs really give you what you want? With us, you can come up with something distinct. If you want to make your home look expensive, you will be glad to hear that it is possible without paying prices. Why would you want to do this? For one thing, it is nice living in a house that has been properly curated. In addition, you can improve the value of your home if you intend to sell up. For those with an interest in going down this path, we have some tips to share.

Panelling

One thing you will want to do is add texture and character with panelling. These days, the panels are revered for how they elevate a space. Originally, it was very popular in period houses. The material gains a degree of gravitas that screams 'expensive' right away if you use it properly. You can have panelling in each room of the property. There are bathroom ideas in addition to living room ones. Furthermore, this is a more cost efficient way of decorating in general. Think about using it on walls and ceilings for the best results.

Replace the carpets with wooden flooring

Something else you can try is replacing carpets with wood flooring. Wooden floors that are timelessly beautiful are a very desirable feature. The wood is revered for its natural, authentic appearance, warmth underfoot, and durability. Flooring is the essential component for setting an interior's tone. When you get this correct, every other detail shall fall into position. Many options exist too, like natural wood, which reveals a character distinction of shades between every board. There are also lighter boards. They produce an element of freshness throughout the year. As a result, they add excellent character to a home.
