The benefits of good architectural design

Words · Coffey Residential · 2 min read

In difficult economic times, there have been arguments that good architectural design is an unaffordable, when in fact the opposite is true. In fractured financial times, the investment in high quality architecture is the wisest possible use of public money. Extensive research has been carried out in the UK and abroad and the results have shown the measurable social and economic benefits of well-designed buildings and spaces. Responsive and thoughtful architecture adds huge value to areas and the people who live and work in them. Design fees are a small fraction of the overall construction costs, and are rendered insignificant when measured against the life of the building. As an investment, good design undoubtedly provides essential aesthetic advantages and long term value for money. Well designed homes and neighbourhoods create healthier and more efficient places for people to live, work and play. The functionality of every building is enhanced and improved by good design. A well designed school positively affects behaviour and supports teaching and learning. Medical facilities such as clinics and hospitals with a well thought out design plan can benefit from speedier processes and efficient and contented staff. Well-designed offices can result in a motivated and productive workforce, and a good quality public space can revitalise neighbourhoods and help to build healthy communities. We are dynamic architects and we work with a diverse range of clients on commercial, public and residential architecture, the UK and overseas. We are dedicated to producing beautifully and ethically designed structures and we collaborate closely with each client to produce the finest solutions and outstanding results. We strive to create uniquely designed buildings that maximise on available space and enhance the lifestyles of all residents and inhabitants. We are happy to assist you with every stage of your project ensuring your every need is met and all that all work is completed within budget and schedule.
