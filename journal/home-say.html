What does your home say about you?

Words · Coffey Residential · 1 min read

A phrase that often resonates with people is 'home is where the heart is'. No matter where you go in the world you will always return to home, the place of comfort, self-expression and safety. Here are some of the important considerations which will always be taken into account when creating a new residential property, or refurbishing an existing one.- Landscaping: The area surrounding your home is what helps to frame and bring together the entire design, as well as being one of the first elements visitors will see.- Efficiency: Energy efficient, sustainable structures express the beliefs of the owners while also saving money on energy bills and minimising your impact on the planet. We are passionate about sustainable architecture and can implement features and strategies to make sure your home complements its environment rather than harming it.- Floor plan: The layout of a property is one of the most vital considerations when it comes to residential architecture. Buildings need to be designed with a sense of flow and purpose, making it easy for people to move through the property and ensuring that they can enjoy living, working and playing in it without feeling constrained. We're an architectural firm that can provide you with a personalised design for your home. Whether you want to extend your home to create extra leisure space or design a completely new building from the ground up we are equipped with the knowledge and experience needed to fulfil all of your requests.
