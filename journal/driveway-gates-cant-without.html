Driveway gates you can't do without

Words · Coffey Residential · 2 min read

A beautiful driveway gate makes an impressive impact. You may want to add a degree of elegance to your house's exterior, create a grand entrance, or keep unwelcome visitors away. Whatever the case, the right gate can tick all the right boxes. There are more than a few options available for driveways. This can make choosing the right gates tough. We're going to explore some choices here to give you an idea of what is available. So, read on to learn more.

Wooden gate

Firstly, we have wooden driveway gates. They have been favourites for a long time because they supply a warm, natural aesthetic. It complements various architectural styles. The customisability is another appealing feature. You can easily stain or paint them to match the house's exterior or produce a striking contrast. These gates will need regular maintenance to protect them from the elements. Saying this, their timeless beauty makes the effort well worth it.

Swing gate

Another choice is the swing gate. These are perhaps the most traditional design for driveways. They work on a simple hinge mechanism. It enables them to swing open like a door. You can design them as single gates that open on one side. Or, they could be double gates that part in the middle. The space for operation is one of the main considerations here; swing gates need ample clearance behind and in front of the gate so they can fully open without obstruction.

Pocket driveway gate

For those with a plan for modern designs or limited space, these gates give an innovative solution. They slide into a recessed area in your property boundary when you open them. As a result, they are ideal for contemporary homes or narrow driveways where you want a minimalist, sleek look. They supply the privacy and security benefits of a classic gate without needing the swing space.

Wrought iron gate

These gates are the epitome of classic elegance. Known for their robust construction and intricate designs, they bring a degree of grandeur to any house. Wrought iron's malleability allows for heavily customised designs. Besides the aesthetic appeal, they provide fantastic security. This is thanks to their durability and strength. Like with wooden gates, you need to do a bit of maintenance, but it's all worth it.
