Suggestions for the guest bedroom

Words · Coffey Residential · 2 min read

Our team of architects works towards meeting the client's requirements in the most efficient ways possible. Using our diverse skills, we can construct a wide variety of homes. If you desire, we can also help you with renovations. When you design them properly, guest bedrooms make everyone feel welcome. To be the ideal host, you should show visitors a charmingly decorated space. If you've yet to design one, you might be a bit stuck for ideas. There's no need to worry however, as we're here to give you some inspiration.

Be thoughtful

One of the last things you'll want to do here is make the space appear to be an afterthought. A way of preventing this is designing an attractive look that pays attention to detail. If you have a rich colour on the walls, it'll cocoon visitors. Make sure you consider lighting, including getting enough natural light and providing enough shade for when people want to sleep.

Quirky

For simple guest rooms, go for a quirky decorating theme. In such a neutral spot, cushions and artwork give the scheme life. Try and set up a mismatching mirror, bedside table, and wooden bedstead to make the space feel lived-in as well as relaxed.

Size

You may think it is okay to set aside the smallest bedroom for your guests. The last thing you want however is for them to feel cramped and uncomfortable. Instead, make sure the room feels spacious and accommodating. If it is an unusual shape try to make the most of it with the right furniture. At Coffey Architects, we can aid you with designing every part of your house. Your plans may focus on the guest rooms or your own personal space. Whatever the case, we'll make certain that you get the most out of your home.
