Building your dream home

Words · Coffey Residential · 2 min read

When you opt to build your new home rather than purchase, you are making a substantial investment in your future. The autonomy offered by a new build allows you to make choices that will perfectly complement your lifestyle. Choosing to construct your new property offers numerous benefits such as modern living spaces that suit your personal needs, open plan spaces and innovative features that are not found in older properties. Building sustainable properties results in more eco-friendly living. You can implement increased water efficiency and energy saving appliances that contribute to a substantial reduction in your energy bills. New homes generally require lower levels of maintenance which helps to free up more of your time for other areas of your life. Our approach to new build projects always incorporates our passion for architecture and its ability to work well with its surroundings. We recently undertook a domestic project that required a proposal for a new four bedroom house. When we assess a location, we take every element into consideration, and with this particular project we considered the precise position of the house between the village and countryside and its relation to the sun. The aim was to secure the sun as the central source of light and heat, and we achieved this by implementing maximum insulation. The inclusion of heat recovery ventilation ensured fresh, clean air. The client required a sustainable property in a familiar location and the house was specifically designed to achieve the code 5 level for sustainable homes. The main focus of the house was enclosure. We designed a secure and warm space akin to a cave that was enveloped by the wild outdoors. The world is your oyster when you build your own home and we offer the perfect solution when looking for innovative residential architecture. Every residential project we take on benefits from our talented and experienced team. We are focused on the connection between people and places, and we take every step to ensure an empathic relationship between your life and the place you live. As architects, we have an inherent responsibility to contribute to the preservation of the planet and the creation of fully functional and sustainable buildings.
