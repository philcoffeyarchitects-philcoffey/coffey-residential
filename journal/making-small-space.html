Making the most of a small space

Words · Coffey Residential · 2 min read

Regardless of your proposal's scale and size, we possess the skills and equipment necessary to complete it with outstanding results. If you are situated within a small apartment, chances are that you're aiming to take full advantage of the room available to you. Typically, nuclear families favour apartments of this calibre as they're both budget-efficient and require relatively minor maintenance. What we're going to discuss here is how more space can be added onto a smaller home to make it appear larger, all without spending too much time or resources. When possible, don't over-pack your spaces. When attempting to decorate a home, design enthusiasts make this typical mistake. If you find that you're unable to walk around without coming into contact with something, then you really need to consider getting rid of some items to free up the space. Integrated storage solutions and using spaces high up walls for shelving and hooks can help to free up the floor too. Remember to utilise vibrant colours as well. The shades present on your interiors play a vital part in deciding its space and aura. The darker your colours, the more compact it'll seem, and vice versa. Vibrant and light shades like pink or yellow make your home look more spacious, so think about using them in some form. At Coffey Architects, we are well known for producing intelligent and wonderfully crafted structures that add value for our clients in a creative manner. We have a lot of experience designing homes and their interiors. Our team shall choreograph the requirements of delivery, deadlines, and budgets, all for the sake of creating a top-notch finish. If there's a special project you have in mind, we hope you'll consider working on it with us. We have passion and always focus on promoting the wellbeing of our clients.
