Bedroom extensions - What to think about before going ahead

Words · Coffey Residential · 2 min read

Your bedroom is the space where you can escape to at the end of a long day. A good night of sleep begins with a welcoming and relaxing room. If it is somewhere cramped where you do not like spending any time at all, it might be worth investing in services for residential architecture for a bedroom extension. With help from trained professionals, the serious job of remodelling your house is much easier. Coffey Architects are able to ensure you end up with a high quality space.

Trends with home extensions

In the UK, one of the most popular forms of work people invest in for their homes is a loft conversion. Even a simple job can do a lot for your space, including raising the value of your home and adding square footage. You can turn the space into whatever you wish, from a spacious bedroom with an en-suite to a home office or one of many other possibilities. If you are looking for an alternative for going up, then how about out? A single storey extension is convenient thanks to its ground floor location. It is also ideal for a bedroom extension or even increasing the size of the living room or kitchen. Whatever you decide on, you are sure to love the results. Furthermore, adding to the room count will have a lasting effect on property value which is great for resale. Of course, not everyone has the space for an outward extension. Not to worry though as there is the option of a garage conversion. These are superb for maximising living space when you hardly use your garage. If you would rather keep this area, then maybe an above garage extension would be perfect for you? This can be very cost effective as you are using your garage which is already there as a foundation.
