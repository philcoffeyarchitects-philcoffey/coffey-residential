What could be popular in interiors in 2024?

Words · Coffey Residential · 2 min read

One of the best things about architecture is that the end of a year gives the chance to look ahead to what could be trending next year. What we want to look at here is things that could be popular in interiors in 2024.

Colours

Firstly, a number of different colours could make a big impact. Overall, more natural shades will probably be the most popular. The anticipation is that chocolate brown will be one of the most common choices for 2024. It is a sultry colour that many people find attractive. As a result, it is a leader for anyone looking at natural colours. Other options like mustard and burnt orange will be great choices too. Blue has been a popular colour for homes for a long time because it has a calming impact. People see it and tend to think about the sea or the sky, feeling a sense of freedom and inspiration. Shades like cobalt, robin's egg, and navy will be great for next year. It has been a top choice for the last few years as people look to connect with nature. It is perfect for interiors because it is soothing. Sage green will likely be the leading choice because it is softer than previous favourites like emerald and jade.

Texture

Another big trend for 2024 will be people choosing materials with texture. It could be a major thing for residential architecture and all other parts of the world. Limewash is a really great example. It is paint that includes crushed lime. Applying it to the walls creates a chalky finish. This can soften colours and make them more enveloping. A number of other textures will be popular too. For example, terracotta could be a great choice. Blond wood has had a resurgence in the last few years too because it has a nice texture with warm tones. Oak, birch, and pine are good choices.

Storage

The last few years has also seen a shift away from digital. More and more people are favouring physical media today, particularly records and books. However, these items need the right storage solutions. So, they will be a crucial part of interiors for 2024. Great looking shelving will have a place in many different rooms. Some people may even go one step further and create an entire library.

Curves

Finally, it seems that 2024 will be a year where people move away from geometric shapes and favour curves more. Irregular shapes can really stand out and make spaces more welcoming. Things like serpentine (or snake) sofas and wishbone chairs are likely to be popular.
