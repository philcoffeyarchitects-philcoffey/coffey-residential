Preparing for a kitchen extension

Words · Coffey Residential · 2 min read

Coffey Architects is a team that has a knack for finding inspiration in the environments surrounding properties. This makes it easier for us to come up with fresh and unconventional ideas. Therefore, you can expect us to complete your project on time and on budget. If you want to produce the home of your dreams, one of the vital steps you'll need to take is planning your kitchen extension. This will provide you with the perfect chance to determine how you could enhance your lifestyle with a few straightforward changes. By following our advice, you'll be able to get the most from the development. What does your extension need? It's integral for your addition to include a spacious prep and cooking zone. You might even want to cater for separate entertaining, relaxing, and dining functions. Generate a list that contains all the attributes currently inside your kitchen. You also need to figure out the location. Your intention could be to produce extra room for a prodigious, open-plan family kitchen. Step one here is to evaluate which part of your house would gain the most from the extension. It would be best for you to take every possibility into account, including amending present layouts and knocking interior walls down. Once you have decided where you want to focus your efforts, make sure you discuss the ideas with an architect. They can tell you what is and isn't feasible. At Coffey Architects, we never lose sight of the ultimate goal whilst participating in a project. By taking an approach to things, we can assist clients in finding that balance required for their property to work. We also strive to ensure every design balances creativity, function and aesthetics. Therefore, if there are details you'd like to discuss with us, feel free to contact our team.
