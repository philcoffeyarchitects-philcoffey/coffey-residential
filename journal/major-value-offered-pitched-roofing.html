The major value offered by pitched roofing

Words · Coffey Residential · 2 min read

It is always surprising just how much value a high quality roof can add to a property. Primarily the surface is an important architectural feature that inspires feelings of comfort and protection. In addition it will provide aesthetic, environmental, and even economic opportunities. Pitched roofs naturally stand out due to the angle. Surprisingly the designs can be immensely diverse, featuring different degrees of pitching, shapes, colours, textures, and even features like gable windows. They can be versatile and innovative, pushing design forward and contributing to the character of the whole property. A number of studies have been done to determine which style of roofing is the best for the environment. Pitched roofs come out as the resounding winner. They are better than flat roofing in terms of energy performance. Thermal efficiency is better too because of the increased ventilation beneath the roofing. The pitched style can also preserve the facade of the property and is more effective at redirecting water. Many of the environmental benefits translate into economic ones. The better thermal performance means less energy is consumed for heating. This can be improved even more with good insulation. The potential additional floorspace can provide a lot of value, particularly because it is a cheaper project than an extension. Most importantly though pitched roofing tends to experience less wear and lasts longer. This reduces replacement costs. Sustainability is a very important factor with modern architecture. Pitched roofs offer a number of advantages in this regard. As mentioned the materials tend to last longer. This is great in terms of the number of resources that are consumed over the property's life. The design can aid in rainwater harvesting, preserving a valuable resource. Many roofing materials used on these surfaces are reusable and recyclable too, including slate and clay tiles. If you want to enjoy aesthetic, environmental, economic, and sustainability benefits, a pitched roof is perfect for your property.
