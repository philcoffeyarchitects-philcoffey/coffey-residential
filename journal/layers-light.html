Layers of light

Words · Coffey Residential · 1 min read

We are a firm that takes on any project given to us by our clients with great enthusiasm. Our goal is always to deliver results, especially when it is your home. We put a huge amount of dedication into our work to give people the very best services. Lighting is vital to producing the correct ambience in a house. What you need is different layers of light. In addition, you will require contrast between lower and higher light levels. When designing a scheme, one of the layers used should be ambient lighting. This offers you a broad base level of functional light. You can then add further layers.

Accent lighting

This will put the focus on the crucial details and features inside a space. Here, you must think about the positioning of your lights. Each one should concentrate on a certain task. For instance, you could put downlights over the taps in your bathroom. This shall supply washing and sparkle walls with light. It can make your smaller rooms seem bigger. At the same time, you will still have decent ambient lighting.

Feature lighting

This is the last layer we will discuss. It includes niche and shelf lighting. Both add detail to a space. Floor and table lamps, and decorative pendants give you the chance to introduce character. A popular approach to interior design is using a neutral palette that has top notch materials and finishes. Moreover, using lights to convey these finishes can amplify their impact.
