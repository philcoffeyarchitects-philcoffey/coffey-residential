Vital considerations to make when it comes to home extensions

Words · Coffey Residential · 2 min read

A common option is the use of house extensions. To ensure you don't face a lot of hassle throughout and end up with something valuable, you have to think various aspects through carefully. Planning and ApplicationsDepending on your situation, you may require a full planning application. With our in-depth knowledge and understanding of building regulations, you can rest easy. We support you along the way. This gives the best chance of obtaining approval as soon as possible for swift progression of the project. Even if you do not need planning permission you still have to follow building regulations. These are there for the safety of the finished space. They consider key aspects such as the structural integrity, energy efficiency, fire safety and more. There are additional considerations to make too, like whether you need a Party Wall Agreement, budgeting and other factors. We can be a helping hand in this situation.How Will It Connect?When building on from an existing property, the design has to flow and match what is already present. Otherwise it can look out of place and fail to add value. Because of this you must use the existing exterior and interior to compliment the new. You must also consider the layout and the impact on plumbing, heating and electrics.Don't Go OverboardPeople can get preoccupied with the idea of extending their building as much as possible. The new space must be useful and you can use other methods alongside it to create a spacious feeling. Clever design ideas can involve using light and making the occupants feel more connected to the outside world. People can also go too far with letting in light. An excess of solar gain can result in an extension only usable for part of the year. The design should take this into consideration. You can diffuse light and glare through the use of fabrics like blinds while adding privacy. In addition you can use insulation to help keep spaces a comfortable temperature.Find An Expert You Can Depend OnThis is a large task that you are taking on so don't go it alone.
