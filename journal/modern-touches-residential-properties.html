Modern touches on residential properties

Words · Coffey Residential · 2 min read

Over a decade in the architecture industry has taught us so much about design and it has enabled us to evolve our services into something that can truly be classified as first-rate. Residential architecture is one of the many types of properties that we have worked with over the years and should you be planning your own project in London, we can help it come to fruition. Modern home designs are generally free of unnecessary elements. Goals that the project needs to meet are clearly defined at the start and only the features that achieve them are included in the design. Residential properties are usually stripped down to showcase the elements present in the home, with the focus being on the space, as opposed to the decor or details irrelevant to the design. Homeowners that follow a modern aesthetic strongly believe in the "less is more" approach. Modern tastes take note of clarity and simplicity, and the best residential properties in this style will be simple, clean and functional. Modernism has varying degrees to it, as some individuals shall opt for the design sense of true minimalism. Others however, favour the idea of integrating minimalistic aesthetics synchronised with their own personal touches. The latter can lead to some truly impressive designs. Instead of concealing the home's nature, modern styles want the viewer to witness the true inner-workings of the project. Nothing is altered in any way to look like something else and the structural elements are commonly revealed to display the supports and structure. This gives a sense of truth in the home, where all the architectural elements and materials are presented honestly. At Coffey Architects we have completed a myriad of residential architecture projects for clients all over London throughout the years. Such projects include but are not limited to, urban lofts, modern mews housing and side extensions.
