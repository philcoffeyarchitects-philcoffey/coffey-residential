The top choices for bathroom flooring

Words · Coffey Residential · 2 min read

There are similarities between the various forms of architecture. However, the distinctions are too great to ignore. Doing so can put your project at risk. To prevent this you need a team of professionals who can help. You can depend on us to assist with your projects.

Lasting bathroom designs

Consider what your bathroom must experience each day. There's moisture, humidity, and also numerous spills. Next, think about what each one can do to your flooring. The floor might not be water resistant. It could also stain badly when you spill a cleaning product or makeup. With all this in mind, you need to think about the most practical flooring choices. We've made it a little easier by looking at some of them below.

Ceramic and porcelain tiles

This kind of tiling is very popular. The latter is cost effective, stylish, and waterproof, everything you need for a great bathroom. These tiles guarantee a solid, textured, and rich look. Merely looking at them summons a sense of sturdiness and elegance. As for ceramic tiles, they're the better option when you'll need to keep spills to a minimum. They work well in bathrooms primarily used as powder rooms or water closets.

Vinyl

This is another potential flooring choice. Your bathroom floor may have to deal with a massive amount of water. If it does, go with vinyl. Since it's available in huge sizes, it might not need any seams in a compact bathroom. It is also very easy to cut to size, making it perfect for rooms with unusual shapes. One of the trendiest choices today is vinyl plank flooring. At Coffey Architects, we can help you to focus on certain parts of your property if that's a concern. Whatever the case, we'll do everything we can to help. As a result we will be with you to ensure we do just that.
