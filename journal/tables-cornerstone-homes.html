Tables are a cornerstone of our homes

Words · Coffey Residential · 2 min read

The greatest thing about quality architecture is you can find it in unique forms in all kinds of locations. We are a firm with a long history of creating distinct designs. Thanks to our help, the properties of our clients have no problem standing out.

Tables bring people together

The table is a pillar of our homes. It creates a space for sharing as well as a centrepiece for dining and living rooms. They allow us to create and reinforce social ties by bringing people together to eat, drink, play games, chat, and more. The table's presence in the home has been a mainstay for centuries. In recent history, the desire for open plan, airiness, and spaciousness, have become a major requirement in designs. Minimalism is a very popular choice for our houses. It is all about producing time and space for moments with our families. We like to replace cleaning with leisure. The absence of dust traps lets the interior breathe too, improving our health. Homeowners shall also be able to move through the open spaces freely, producing a sense of freedom. The presence of one or two unique feature pieces shall enliven these areas considerably. A focal point like this could be a beautiful table. They can fulfil a multitude of functions and also complement the interior without interfering.

The pride of the house

Tables can also be a main piece of decoration. You can choose a design that reflects your tastes and personality, whether it is a sleek metal and glass model or a traditional wooden one with benches. Also, the size and shape can vary depending on harmony with the environment and functionality. Bigger kitchen tables can become more compact coffee tables in our living rooms. In the bedroom, they become bedside tables. The range of shapes can add a different dimension to any room too. With the right chairs or a sofa, the table can produce an oasis of calm for sitting with family, friends, and visitors.
