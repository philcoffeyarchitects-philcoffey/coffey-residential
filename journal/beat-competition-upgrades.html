Beat the competition with a few upgrades

Words · Coffey Residential · 2 min read

One thing many people don't realise about architecture is that it does not have to look normal. You may have some distinct ideas of your own. If so, then ours is the team that can help them come to life. Competition for real estate can be great at the best of times. However, in some situations it can cause issues. You may have been having some trouble getting prospective buyers or renters to notice your property. You can make it stand out with a few upgrades here and there. The following are things you should consider. Better still, these are ones you can make on a budget without changing the structure.

Fixtures

Start by upgrading the water fixtures. There are few things worse than having old and disgusting taps in the kitchen and bathroom. If you have any, update them for some new and shiny ones. Ensure that they have the same finish and that they all match too. There are many hardware options available so you should not have any problem here.

Backsplashes

You can also try adding in a backsplash. They can be a great addition to sinks in a kitchen or bathroom. The backsplashes are not always incredibly expensive but they can really enhance rooms. Unique glass products or immaculate tiles are usually the best choices. You can use them to create a kitchen and bathroom that stands out.

Greenery

One last thing we recommend is adding some plants. The greenery can really make all the difference. What it can do is give your property a pristine and polished look. It also improves the atmosphere and sense of actually living in the space. There are lots of different plants to choose from, just make sure you check the growing conditions so they thrive.
