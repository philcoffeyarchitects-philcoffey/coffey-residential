Considerations to make when building a garage

Words · Coffey Residential · 1 min read

From extensions and refurbishments to the construction of penthouses and museums, we have completed a monumental number of projects. Garages can prove to be a valuable addition to your home. Not only are they colossally convenient, but they offer future redevelopment potential too. Where you focus your efforts shall be determined by your house's layout. Furthermore, your garage's construction demands that you consider certain factors. We will discuss some of them here. Every construct needs a foundation to stabilise it. A garage typically requires a concrete one. For you, this means back-fill and excavation. Due to the complex nature of this work, it's best to leave it in the hands of professionals such as us. The surface must be strong and durable, ensuring it can bear the load placed on it. Power is a necessity for almost every garage. There are also chances that some degree of plumbing shall be needed too. It is better to have amenities installed at the outset than doing it retroactively. At Coffey Architects, we see to it that our architectural pieces meet all of the client's long-term requirements. With our creations, you shall be able to make the most of your property without upsetting its balance. We can carefully combine aesthetics and function, ensuring the perfect result is achieved. We are proud to contribute to this with our designs.
