Considering a home with a balcony? Go for it!

Words · Coffey Residential · 2 min read

Coffey Architects has won several awards by creating residential architecture residents love. We take on jobs from small extensions to new builds. You have a lot of choices to make when you design a new home. There are so many ways to personalise it and you can pick from numerous features like a balcony.

Enjoy your surroundings

Of course, one of the best things a balcony will bring to your new space is an amazing view. Rather than gazing from a window, you will be able to step into the outdoors to embrace your surroundings. This gives you the chance to see a broader picture of the panoramic landscape around you.

Bring the outdoors in

Smaller balconies tend to have their own mini garden or a table and chairs to give owners the chance to sit outside and relax. Those that are big enough can even be the perfect spot for a get-together.

Let the sun in

Another reason that balconies are fantastic is that they will provide your property with a lot more natural light. This is perfect if the building doesn't get a lot of sunlight on one side. Using doors with floor to ceiling panes rather than a window guarantees that the room will get a lot more sun.

An airy space

You will likely know from experience that it can sometimes be hard to get air to parts of a property, especially when there is only a small window. A balcony could prove to be the perfect solution to this problem. It will supply your space with more air by creating a bigger draught when you open the doors. This is even more helpful in summer because it can alleviate a hot and stuffy space with a natural breeze.
