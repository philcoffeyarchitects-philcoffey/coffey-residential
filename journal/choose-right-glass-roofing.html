How to choose the right glass roofing

Words · Coffey Residential · 2 min read

More and more people are looking at glass roofing for their homes. It is a great alternative to a solid roof because it can offer lots of benefits. There are also several different options so you can choose the right one for you. Coffey Architects can help you choose the right elements for every part of your home.

Benefits

For starters it has excellent thermal efficiency. Perhaps more importantly, it lets lots of light into a space. This means you can have rooms that are bright and welcoming. The glazing will also last a long time.

Glass roofing options

There are lots of different styles of glass roofing to consider. The first is to glaze the entire surface. You can choose all kinds of designs here, including shallow and deep pitches, as well as styles like Victorian and Georgian. The roof will have a metal frame to support the glazing. If you don't want a full glass roof you can choose one of several types of smaller glazed elements. For example you could opt for skylights, lanterns, pyramids, or even light wells. You can even combine several of these to get the result you want. Whatever style you decide it is crucial you get the right kind of glazing. You must specify a type of glass that will get you the thermal gain and performance you want. You should also think about the coatings, privacy, cleaning, and ventilation. Again there are lots of options in each category.

Building Regulations and planning permission

Whatever you are planning, it is always important to think about these two things. The last thing you want is to find out your choice of roof does not meet the regulations. This could result in a redesign. The same applies if you cannot get planning permission. This is possible if you are making big changes to an existing property. It can also happen if you are building something new that is remarkably different to the other properties in the area.
