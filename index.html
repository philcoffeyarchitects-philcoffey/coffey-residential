About · The Studio

We design houses around light.

Coffey Residential is a small London studio of architects. Phil Coffey founded the practice in 2005, and twenty years on, he is on every project.

The questions that interest us are quiet ones. How a room reads at half past four in February. How a stair takes the morning. What a house feels like in five years, and in fifty.

Services

What we do.

Read about our process  →
I

Design

Concept and scheme design. Feasibility, briefing, sketch ideas through to a resolved architectural proposition for your house.

II

Planning

Planning applications, listed building consent, pre-application advice and conservation area work. London boroughs are our home turf.

III

Detail

Technical design and detailing — every junction, joint and finish drawn at one-to-one. The drawings the builder actually needs.

IV

Project management

Tendering, contract administration and site supervision. We stay on site from first dig to final snag, weekly.
